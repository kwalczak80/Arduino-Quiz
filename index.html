Rules of this quiz are very simple:

  • During the quiz, you will be asked to answer ten questions related to the Arduino microcontroller with four possible answers to each question.
  • Select the answer you think is the correct answer.
  • Each question must be answered to move to the next question.
  • Once the answer is selected hit the Submit button to move to the next question.
  • You can check your answers in the scoreboard under the submit button after you answer the question.
  • Remember - there are no time restrictions so take as much time as you need to to complete this quiz.
  • Best of luck !!
